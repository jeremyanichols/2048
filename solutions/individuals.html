We’re in it for the long haul

We are driven by the mission of decentralization and securing the blockchain. This began with our Founder & CEO, Chun Wang. Chun's journey in the blockchain space started in 2013 as a co-founder of f2pool, a leading mining pool for Bitcoin, Ethereum, and other Proof of Work (PoW) protocols.

In 2018, Chun launched stakefish as a pioneering Proof of Stake (PoS) staking pool, marking a new chapter in our mission. Today, stakefish stands as one of the largest and most trusted validators, managing over $3 billion in staked assets.

Open sourced our batch contract, and used by prominent platforms.
